Panoramic airplane flights – a unique view of Croatia

Panoramic flights over Zagreb and the surrounding area offer an experience that few people ever get to have: a view from above that changes your perspective. In a small sports aircraft, accompanied by an experienced licensed pilot, you rise into the air and discover just how beautiful Croatia truly is — from the green hills of Zagorje to the sparkling waters of the Adriatic. Panoramic airplane flights are not reserved for pilots or adventurous souls — they are open to anyone who wants something different and unforgettable.

What makes this airplane flight over Croatia so special is its flexibility. Every route is tailored to your wishes: you can fly above the castles and vineyards of Zagorje, follow the coast towards Kvarner, or simply enjoy the panorama of Zagreb and Medvednica. There is no fixed schedule or crowds — the flight is yours, from takeoff to landing.

FlyWay Aeroclub has years of experience organising panoramic flights for couples, families, photographers and businesses. Our pilot Uroš Todorović Mikšaj ensures safety and a comfortable experience, while you relax and enjoy views you will never forget. If you are looking for a panoramic airplane flight in Zagreb, you have come to the right place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior flying experience?

No, all you need is to relax and enjoy. The experienced pilot takes care of everything — from takeoff to landing.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least one week in advance, especially for weekends and public holidays when demand is higher.

What if the weather is bad?

Safety is our number one priority. In the event of unfavourable weather conditions, we arrange a new date at no charge.

Can I record or take photos?

Absolutely! Feel free to bring cameras and phones. The views you will capture are worth a thousand words.

Is a gift voucher available?

Yes, we offer gift vouchers for panoramic airplane flights — a perfect gift that will be remembered.
