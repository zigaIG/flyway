What is tandem paragliding?

Tandem paragliding is a form of free flight in which an experienced licensed pilot and a passenger fly together under a single wing — a paraglider. You sit in a comfortable harness seat in front of or below the pilot, who is in full control of the glider. All you need to do is enjoy the flight and the views stretching out below you. Paragliding Zagreb combines the excitement of free flight with exceptional safety thanks to strict pilot licensing standards.

The Zagorje and Medvednica area is one of the most beautiful in all of Croatia for tandem paragliding. Gentle terrain, diverse launch sites at altitudes between 500 and 1000 metres, and reliable winds in spring and autumn make this area ideal for paragliding flights around Zagreb. From the air you get incredible views of castles, vineyards, green valleys and — on a clear day — all the way to the Alps and the Adriatic Sea.

Tandem paragliding in Croatia requires no prior flying experience whatsoever. Our pilots have completed a rigorous training programme and hold all the necessary licences, and they plan every flight in detail according to weather conditions. All you need to bring is a good mood — we take care of everything else.

How does a tandem flight work?

  • 1️⃣We contact you and agree on a date and launch site based on the weather forecast — safety always comes first.
  • 2️⃣You arrive at the launch site — all paragliding equipment is provided, no need to bring anything special except appropriate clothing and footwear.
  • 3️⃣A brief briefing with the pilot about what will happen — step by step, without rushing, until you feel completely comfortable.
  • 4️⃣You run a few steps into the slope and — takeoff! The glider lifts you into the air almost imperceptibly smoothly.
  • 5️⃣You enjoy a flight of 10 to 30 minutes above the spectacular landscape of Zagorje, Medvednica or the coast, depending on the chosen launch site.
  • 6️⃣A soft landing on a pre-prepared surface — the pilot guides you step by step all the way to a safe touchdown.

Launch Sites

The choice of launch site depends on weather conditions and wind direction on the day of the flight. Our pilots always choose the location that guarantees the safest and most memorable flight.

🪂

Launch Sites — Paragliding Zagreb area

  • Japetić (879 m a.s.l.) Most popular launch site near Samobor, views of Samoborsko gorje and the Pannonian Plain
  • Ivanščica (1061 m a.s.l.) Highest peak of Croatian Zagorje, long and spectacular flights
  • Ravna Gora (Zagorje) Peaceful launch site with views over Zagorje
  • Kalnik Scenic hills between Koprivnica and Varaždin
  • Tribalj (Vinodol) Mediterranean launch site with views of the Adriatic Sea

What to bring?

  • 👖Long clothing (long trousers and long-sleeved top) — mandatory
  • 👟Sturdy ankle boots (hiking boots or similar) — mandatory
  • 🧥Comfortable clothing suitable for physical activity (it can be cold at altitude)
  • 💧Water and a light snack, especially for morning flights
  • 😊Good mood!

All paragliding equipment (glider, harness, helmet) is provided by the club.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience?

No, tandem paragliding requires no prior experience whatsoever. The pilot handles everything — from takeoff to landing. Your only job is to enjoy.

What if I am afraid of heights?

Many guests have a fear of heights but still enjoy the flight — the feeling is completely different from looking down from a cliff. We recommend relaxing and trusting the pilot.

Can a child fly?

Yes, with written parental consent and if the child is at least 8 years old. For children under 16, a parent or guardian must be present at the launch site.

What if the weather is bad?

The flight does not take place in strong winds, rain or poor visibility. In case of unfavourable conditions we arrange a new date at no charge — safety is always the priority.

Can I bring a camera?

Yes, you can use an action camera (GoPro etc.) by arrangement with the pilot. The camera must be securely attached so it does not interfere with the flight.
