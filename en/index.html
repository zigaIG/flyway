Flights

Tandem Paragliding

Experience the thrill of paragliding with an experienced tandem pilot. No prior experience needed — we take care of everything!

  • 👕Long sleeves, long pants and hiking boots required
  • 🎒All equipment provided by the club
  • 🎥Flights can be recorded with action cameras
  • 👦Minors allowed with written parental consent

Tandem paragliding in Zagreb — launch sites Japetić, Ivanščica and Kalnik, spectacular views of Zagorje and Medvednica. No experience needed, from age 8.

Panoramic Airplane Tours

Enjoy spectacular aerial views on customizable routes. Groups of up to three passengers, departing from Varaždin or Lučko aerodrome.

  • ✈️Customizable routes of your choice
  • 👥Groups of up to 3 passengers
  • 📍Varaždin & Lučko aerodrome

Panoramic airplane flights from Varaždin or Lučko airport — customized routes over Croatia, up to 3 passengers. Perfect for special occasions and an unforgettable gift.

Safety

🛡️

Licensed Pilots

All our pilots hold valid licenses and regularly undergo checks and training according to the rules of the Croatian Aeronautical Association.

🪂

Certified Equipment

We use exclusively certified paragliding and aviation equipment that is regularly serviced and replaced according to prescribed schedules.

📋

Insurance

Every flight is covered by mandatory aviation insurance. Your safety and peace of mind are our number one priority.
